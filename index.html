    Machi Koro Rules

    Each player starts with two establishments (one Wheat Field and one Bakery) and 3 coins.
    You will also have four Landmark cards (Train Station, Shopping Mall, Amusement Park, Radio Tower) that will be under construction.

    Game Flow

      A turn consists of the following three phases:

    • Roll Dice: to begin their turn a player rolls the dice. When rolling two dice, the dice are always summed together.

    • Earn Income: Players earn income based on the dice roll and the effects of the Establishments that they own that match the dice roll.
        • There are 3 different types of establishments:
      • BLUE: Get income from the bank on ANYONE'S turn.
      • GREEN: Get income from the bank ONLY on your turn.
      • RED: Take coins from the person that rolled the dice. As long as that person has coins to give.

      **If you own multiple copies of a single Establishment, the effects are multiplied by the number of Establishments of that type owned.


    • Construction: Buy Establishments or Landmarks from the marketplace after you rolled the dice and earn and/or give income. You can only buy one Establishment or Landmark on your turn. Or you may choose to end your turn.

      • Game End: The player to construct all four of their Landmarks first wins the game!
